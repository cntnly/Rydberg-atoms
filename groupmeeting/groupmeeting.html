Effective rate equation

 
$$\dot{\rho} = i(\sum_j^N[\mathcal{H}_j, \rho] + [\mathcal{H}_{int}, \rho])+\sum_\nu (1/2)(2L_\nu \rho L_\nu^\dagger - \{L_\nu^\dagger L_\nu,\rho\}) $$ where $$\mathcal{H}_j = (\frac{\Omega}{2}| r\rangle\langle g |_j + h.c) + \Delta | r \rangle\langle r | $$ $$\mathcal{H}_{int} = VdW$$ $$L_\nu = \text{jump operator}$$


Non interacting atoms:
$$\begin{align} \dot{\rho}_{gg} & = -\frac{i}{2} \Omega (\rho_{gr} - \rho_{rg}) + \Gamma_{sp} \rho_{rr}\\ \dot{\rho}_{rr} & = +\frac{i}{2} \Omega (\rho_{gr} - \rho_{rg}) - (\Gamma_{ion} + \Gamma_{sp}) \rho_{rr}\\ \dot{\rho}_{gr} & = -\left(\frac{\Gamma_{ion} +\Gamma_{sp} +\Gamma_{d}}{2} + i\Delta\right)\rho_{gr} + \frac{i}{2}\Omega (\rho_{rr} - \rho_{gg}) \\ \dot{\rho}_{ion} & = \Gamma_{ion}\rho_{rr} \end{align} $$ where: $$\Gamma_{ion} = 45 \text{ kHz},\, \Gamma_{sp} = 5 \text{ kHz} $$ $$\Gamma_d : \text{decoherence rate (laser, thermal motion, electric field, ...)}$$
$$\dot{\rho}_{gr} \xrightarrow{\Gamma_d \gg 1} 0, \; \gamma=\Gamma_{ion} + \Gamma_{sp} +\Gamma_d :$$ $$\dot{\rho}_{rr} = -\left(\frac{\Omega^2 \gamma}{\gamma^2 + 4\Delta^2} + \Gamma_{ion} + \Gamma_{sp}\right)\rho_{rr} + \frac{\Omega^2 \gamma}{\gamma^2 + 4\Delta^2}\rho_{gg} \quad (*)$$ $$\text{ peak ion at } \dot{\rho_{rr}=0} \longrightarrow \rho_{rr}\approx \frac{1}{\frac{\Omega^2 \gamma}{\gamma^2 + 4\Delta^2} +\Gamma_{ion} + \Gamma_{sp}}\frac{\Omega^2 \gamma}{\gamma^2 + 4\Delta^2} \rho_{gg} $$ $$I_{\Omega \ll \gamma} = \rho_{ion}N_{atoms} = \frac{\Gamma_{ion}N_{atoms}}{\Gamma_{ion} + \Gamma_{sp}}\frac{\Omega^2 \gamma}{\gamma^2 + 4\Delta^2} $$

Interacting atoms:


 
$(*)$ is valid for $\Gamma_d \gg 1$ with individual detuning $$ \delta_i = \Delta + \sum_j \frac{C_6^{\text{eff}}}{|r_i - r_j|^6} |r\rangle\langle r|_j$$
